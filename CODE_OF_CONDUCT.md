# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity or expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity or orientation, or technology choices.

## Expected behavior

Contributors are expected to:

- use welcoming and inclusive language;
- respect differing viewpoints and experiences;
- give and accept constructive feedback;
- focus on what is best for the community and project;
- protect privacy and never pressure others to disclose sensitive RFID data; and
- act in good faith when discussing security, compatibility, or safety concerns.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory remarks, personal attacks, deliberate intimidation, publishing private information without permission, or posting real RFID/NFC credentials and data that could affect another person or system.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainers. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as fairly and confidentially as practical. Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or take other proportionate action to protect participants and the project.
